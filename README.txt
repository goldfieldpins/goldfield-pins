GOLDFIELDPINS.COM STARTER SITE

1. Open index.html in a browser to preview the website.
2. Replace the placeholder product and gallery blocks with your own images.
3. In index.html, change:
   - $XX.XX to the pin price
   - YOUR-EMAIL@example.com to your email
   - The Buy Now href="#" to your Stripe, PayPal, or Square payment link
4. Upload the entire folder to Netlify Drop or GitHub Pages.
5. Connect GoldfieldPins.com through your hosting provider's custom-domain settings.

The site includes:
- Responsive one-page storefront
- Logo and matching favicon
- Current product section
- Past pin gallery
- Shipping, returns, privacy, and terms pages
